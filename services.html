Services

PRE CONSTRUCTION PLANNING & CONSULTING

As Consultants, Wells Builders provides invaluable services during your projects planning stage including site Investigations, document review, scheduling, budget development, and permitting assistance

CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT

As Construction Managers, Wells Builders becomes your team member throughout the construction delivery process. Whether you choose for Wells to become the owner’s agent and you hold all the trade contracts, or as your CM “At Risk” where we become fully responsible for the delivery of your project, we allow you to leverage our expertise in an open book fashion to deliver you value, on time performance, and most importantly quality

GENERAL CONTRACTING

As a General Contractor, Wells can join your team in the traditional “design-bid-build” delivery format or as a member of your team on a negotiated basis

DESIGN / BUILD SERVICES

As a Design/Builder, Wells acts as your single point of contact for total project delivery. This method provides our clients with an efficient way to obtain timely, cost effective project delivery
